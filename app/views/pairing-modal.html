About CoderCombat

Underscoreboard is a realtime coding game where players try to implement an Underscore (a JavaScript library) function faster than their opponent. Players get realtime feedback in the form of a test suite that updates as they type. Make all the tests pass to win!

If you're just here to check it out, click here to open a second tab to play by yourself.
